Why The Woodlands chooses Jamail

The drive's worth it — and we make it weekly

The Woodlands is 45 minutes north of our Colquitt Street showroom, and a lot of local flooring shops can't or won't make that drive for an in-home consultation. We do — every week. Our trucks are up I-45 several times a week, and we keep installation crews working in the area on rotation, which means we can quote, schedule, and start a project in The Woodlands almost as quickly as one inside the Beltway.

We've installed in 40+ Woodlands homes — from forest-edged custom builds in Carlton Woods to remodels in the original villages. The Woodlands tends to draw two project profiles: new-construction owners adding hardwood as a builder upgrade, and longtime residents replacing 90s carpet or tile with wide-plank oak. We handle both.
