Why River Oaks chooses Jamail

Built for the homes that defined Houston

River Oaks homes are different. Many are pre-war or mid-century originals being lovingly restored. Others are recent rebuilds where the architecture is contemporary but the expectation is still craft. Either way, the floors matter — and most national chains aren't equipped for the level of detail River Oaks projects demand.

We've installed European white oak in 60+ River Oaks homes since 2015. We understand the architectural standards, the HOA quirks, the parking constraints, and the level of finish work that residents and their designers expect. Most of our River Oaks work comes from referrals — from clients, from interior designers, and from neighbors who saw the truck and the moving blankets and asked who we were.

What working with us looks like

How we run a River Oaks project

1

In-home consultation

We come to you. Look at the rooms, the light, the existing finishes. Bring sample boards and answer the questions you should be asking. Free, no follow-up pressure.

2

Discrete, dust-controlled install

We're used to occupied homes. HEPA dust containment, daily cleanup, blue-tape protection of trim and stone. We've worked alongside other trades on multi-phase renovations without ever being the problem.

3

Long-term relationship

We're here. When you have a question about the floor a year later — a scratch, a refinish, a question about a wet rug — call us. We don't disappear after the check clears.
